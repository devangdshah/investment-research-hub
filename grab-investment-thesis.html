Grab's Business Model: The Everyday Everything App

Grab operates on a comprehensive super app model, integrating a wide range of services to meet the daily needs of consumers and businesses across Southeast Asia. Its diverse revenue streams underpin its robust ecosystem.

Core Business Segments

Deliveries

Encompasses food delivery (GrabFood), grocery delivery (GrabMart), and package delivery (GrabExpress). A key growth driver with high user frequency.

Mobility

Includes ride-hailing (GrabCar, GrabBike, GrabTaxi) and other transportation solutions, leveraging an extensive network of driver-partners.

Financial Services

Offers digital payment solutions (GrabPay), lending, insurance, and digital banking services (GXS Bank, GXBank), deepening user engagement and monetization.

Enterprise & New Initiatives

Includes GrabAds (advertising platform for businesses), Grab for Business, and other emerging ventures.
