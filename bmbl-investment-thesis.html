Bumble's Business Model: Freemium & Monetization

Bumble operates on a freemium business model, providing free core dating and social networking features while generating revenue through premium subscriptions and in-app purchases.

Core Product & Service Offerings

Bumble App (Dating)

The flagship female-first dating app where women make the first move. Offers features like "Opening Moves" for enhanced conversation starters.

Bumble For Friends (BFF)

A mode within the Bumble app (or standalone) focused on helping users find platonic friendships and build social connections.

Bumble Bizz (Networking)

A professional networking mode enabling users to connect for career opportunities, mentorship, and business collaborations.

Badoo App

A globally popular dating app, operating on a similar freemium model, offering a broader approach to meeting new people.
