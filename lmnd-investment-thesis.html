Revenue Streams & Giveback Model

Lemonade generates revenue primarily from insurance premiums. A portion of unused premiums is donated to charity through its "Giveback" program.

  • **Earned Premiums:** Primary revenue from policies sold across all product lines.
  • **Reinsurance:** Ceding a portion of premiums and risk to reinsurers, reducing volatility.
  • **Fixed Fee Model:** Lemonade takes a fixed fee from premiums (initially 20%, evolved structure) to cover expenses and profit, before claims and Giveback.
  • **Giveback Program:** Unclaimed premiums (after claims and fixed fee) are donated to non-profits selected by customers, fostering trust and loyalty.
